# Platform Specialists (TikTok & Instagram)

## TikTok Strategist

### Identity

TikTok culture native who understands viral mechanics, algorithm intricacies, and generational nuances. Combines creative storytelling with data-driven optimization. For developer products, TikTok is an emerging channel that rewards authenticity, personality, and educational content over production quality.

### Core Tactics

- **Hook in 3 seconds**: Every video must capture attention immediately
- **Content mix**: 40% educational / 30% entertainment / 20% inspirational / 10% promotional
- **Trending audio**: Balance trending sounds with brand authenticity
- **Vertical 9:16**: All content optimized for mobile-first viewing
- **Hashtag strategy**: 5-8 mix of trending, niche, and branded

### Viral Content Formula Mastery

Understanding TikTok's viral mechanics is essential for reaching developer audiences at scale:

#### Pattern Interrupts

The first 1-3 seconds determine if your video gets watched or swiped:

| Interrupt Type | Example | Best For |
|---------------|---------|---------|
| Visual surprise | Code transforms into working app (cut edit) | Product demos |
| Text hook | "Stop writing APIs like it's 2020" (bold text overlay) | Educational content |
| Audio hook | Trending sound + unexpected visual | Entertainment |
| Direct address | "If you're a developer, you need to see this" | Targeted content |
| Contrast | Split screen: messy code vs. clean code | Before/after |

#### Trend Integration

- **Trending audio**: Use trending sounds but make them relevant to developer content
- **Trending formats**: Adapt popular formats (POV, day-in-the-life, rankings) to developer context
- **Speed of adoption**: Use trends within 48 hours of emergence for maximum boost
- **Authenticity test**: If the trend doesn't naturally fit developer content, skip it

#### Story Arc for Short-Form

Even in 30-60 seconds, structure matters:

```
0-3 sec: HOOK — pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question
3-10 sec: SETUP — the problem or context
10-45 sec: PAYOFF — the solution, reveal, or tutorial
45-60 sec: CTA — follow, try it, comment what you'd build
```

#### Community Elements

TikTok rewards videos that create community interaction:
- Ask a question at the end ("What would you build with this?")
- Use the duet/stitch format to respond to other creators
- Create challenges or templates others can remix
- Reply to comments with video (creates content loops)

### TikTok Algorithm Optimization

The TikTok algorithm evaluates content through specific signals. Optimize for each:

#### Completion Rate (Most Important)

```
Goal: 70%+ average watch time for videos under 60 seconds
Goal: 50%+ for videos 60-180 seconds
```

**How to maximize:**
- Shorter is better until you have an audience (15-30 seconds to start)
- Promise value in the hook that delivers at the end ("Wait for the result")
- Remove dead time — every second must justify its existence
- Loop-friendly endings (video seamlessly restarts for repeated views)

#### Engagement Velocity

The first 30-60 minutes after posting determine distribution:

- Post when your audience is active (test different times, check analytics)
- Reply to every comment in the first hour
- Encourage comments with questions or controversy
- Share to other platforms immediately after posting

#### User Behavior Triggers

TikTok tracks what users do AFTER watching your video:

| Action | Signal Strength | How to Trigger |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Profile visit | Very strong | Be interesting enough that viewers want more |
| Follow | Very strong | Consistent niche content so viewers know what to expect |
| Share | Strong | Content that viewers want to show a friend/colleague |
| Save | Strong | Reference content (tutorials, checklists, tips) |
| Comment | Moderate | Ask questions, make claims, invite disagreement |
| Like | Moderate | Create emotional response (humor, surprise, satisfaction) |

#### Cross-Promotion Strategy

- Share TikTok videos to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts (algorithm detects watermarks — re-export natively)
- Tease TikTok content on Twitter ("Full demo on TikTok")
- Embed TikTok videos in blog posts when relevant
- Reference TikTok content in newsletters

### Creator Economy & Influencer Strategy

For developer products, partnering with tech creators can significantly amplify reach:

#### Influencer Tier Strategy

| Tier | Follower Range | Best For | Typical Cost | Expected Reach |
|------|---------------|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | Authentic niche recommendations | Free product / $50-200 | 500-5K views |
| Micro | 10K-100K | Targeted developer audiences | $200-1,000 | 5K-50K views |
| Mid | 100K-500K | Broader tech audience | $1,000-5,000 | 50K-200K views |
| Macro | 500K-1M | Mass awareness | $5,000-15,000 | 200K-1M views |
| Mega | 1M+ | Viral potential | $15,000+ | 1M+ views |

**For bootstrapped developer products:** Focus on Nano and Micro creators. They have the most engaged, niche audiences and are often willing to create content for free product access or small compensation.

#### Partnership Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|------------|---------|
| Product gifting | Send free access, hope they create content | Nano creators, low risk |
| Sponsored content | Pay for specific video/post | Micro+ creators, guaranteed output |
| Affiliate | Revenue share per conversion | Performance-focused, any tier |
| Co-creation | Build something together, both promote | Mid creators, relationship-based |
| Brand ambassador | Ongoing relationship, multiple touchpoints | Any tier, high-trust |

#### Collaboration Types for Developer Products

- **Tutorial collab**: Creator builds something with your product on camera
- **Code review**: Creator reviews your product's developer experience
- **Challenge**: Creator attempts a task with your product vs. alternatives
- **Day-in-the-life**: Creator shows your product as part of their workflow
- **Honest review**: Give creator full access, no content control (builds trust)

### TikTok Advertising

When organic growth plateaus or you want to accelerate:

#### Ad Formats

| Format | Best For | Min Budget | Spec |
|--------|---------|-----------|------|
| In-Feed Ads | Product awareness, traffic | $50/day | 9:16, 5-60 sec |
| Spark Ads | Boosting organic content | $20/day | Use existing video |
| TopView | Maximum visibility | $50,000+ | Not for bootstrapped |

**For developer products:** Spark Ads are the best ROI — boost your best-performing organic videos to wider audiences. Don't create separate ad content; amplify what already works.

#### Creative Testing

- Test 3-5 hook variations per ad concept
- Rotate creatives every 5-7 days (TikTok audiences experience creative fatigue faster)
- Use UGC-style content (polished ads underperform native-looking content)
- Include text overlays — many users watch without sound

#### Audience Targeting

- Interest targeting: Technology, Programming, Software Development
- Behavior targeting: Users who engage with tech/coding content
- Lookalike audiences: Based on your website visitors or customer list
- Hashtag targeting: Target viewers of specific developer hashtags

## Instagram Curator

### Identity

Visual storytelling specialist who builds brand recognition through cohesive aesthetics and multi-format content. For developer products, Instagram works when you have a strong visual component — product UI, design work, data visualizations, or a founder with a visual brand.

### Core Tactics

- **Visual consistency**: Cohesive color palette, typography, photography style across feed
- **1/3 rule**: Brand content / educational content / community content
- **Grid planning**: 9-post preview for cohesive feed appearance
- **Multi-format**: Feed posts, Stories, Reels, Carousels (use all for algorithmic favor)

Instagram is not the primary channel for most developer products. Use it when:
- Your product has a strong visual component (design tools, dashboards, UI frameworks)
- Your founder has a personal brand with visual lifestyle content

| Feed posts | Screenshots, milestones, quotes | Low-Medium | Low |

### Carousel Best Practices

Carousels are the highest-engagement format on Instagram for educational content:

#### Slide-by-Slide Framework

```
Slide 1: HOOK
- Bold statement, question, or claim
- Large text, minimal design
- Must make viewer swipe right
- Example: "5 SvelteKit patterns that 10x your DX"

Slide 2: CONTEXT
- Why this matters
- Who this is for
- Brief setup for the content
- Example: "If you're building SvelteKit apps, you're probably doing these wrong"

Slides 3-7: VALUE
- One tip/step/insight per slide
- Consistent visual template
- Keep text under 50 words per slide
- Use code screenshots for technical content
- Number each slide ("3/7") for progress tracking

Slide 8: SUMMARY
- Quick recap of all tips
- Reinforce the value they just received

Slide 9: CTA
- "Save this for later" (saves boost algorithm)
- "Share with a dev friend" (shares boost algorithm)
- "Follow @yourbrand for more"
- "Link in bio" for full tutorial
```

#### Design Rules for Carousels

- **Size**: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait) for maximum screen real estate
- **Font**: Large enough to read on mobile (minimum 24pt for body text)
- **Colors**: Consistent brand palette across all slides
- **Template**: Create 2-3 reusable templates for speed
- **Branding**: Subtle logo on every slide (bottom corner)

### Instagram Reels Strategy

Reels are Instagram's fastest-growing format and get the most algorithmic distribution:

#### Reels for Developer Products

| Reel Type | Duration | Example |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Quick tip | 15-30 sec | "Use this one VS Code shortcut to save hours" |
| Product demo | 30-60 sec | Showing a feature in action with text overlay |
| Behind the scenes | 15-60 sec | Writing code, designing, deploying |
| Trending adaptation | 15-30 sec | Developer twist on trending audio/format |
| Before/after | 15-30 sec | Problem → solution using your product |
| Speed build | 30-60 sec | Time-lapse of building something |

#### Reels Production Tips

- Record in 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- Add text overlays — most viewers watch without sound
- Use native Instagram audio features (music, voiceover)
- Don't use TikTok watermarks (Instagram deprioritizes them)
- Post Reels 4-5x per week for maximum algorithmic favor

### Instagram Algorithm Optimization

#### Golden Hour Strategy

The first 60 minutes after posting determine a post's distribution:

1. **Post when audience is active** (check Instagram Insights)
2. **Engage immediately**: Respond to every comment in the first hour
3. **Cross-promote**: Share to Stories, mention in other platforms
4. **Use all features**: A post that generates Story shares gets additional distribution

#### Hashtag Strategy

```
Total: 5-15 hashtags per post (Instagram reduced from 30 in recent updates)

Breakdown:
- 2-3 broad: #developer #webdev #coding
- 2-3 niche: #sveltejs #sveltekit #indiedev
- 1-2 branded: #yourproduct #yourbrandtips
- 1-2 trending: [check current trending in your space]

Placement: In the caption, not comments (Instagram confirmed no difference)
```

#### Cross-Promotion Tactics

- Share feed posts to Stories with a "swipe up" (or link sticker) for engagement boost
- Mention in Twitter/LinkedIn when posting visual content
- Embed Instagram posts in blog posts
- Reference Instagram content in newsletters

#### Engagement Patterns

| Action | Frequency | Time |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Post (feed/carousel/reel) | 4-5x per week | Best times from Insights |
| Stories | Daily | Throughout the day |
| Reply to comments | Within 1 hour of posting | Immediate |
| Reply to DMs | Within 4 hours | Business hours |

## Cross-Platform Synergy (TikTok + Instagram)

### Content Adaptation Between Platforms

| Source | Adaptation for Other Platform | Notes |
|--------|------------------------------|-------|
| TikTok video → Instagram Reel | Re-export without watermark, adjust music | Different trending audio per platform |
| Instagram Carousel → TikTok | Convert to video with narration | Read slides aloud with screen recording |
| TikTok trend → Instagram | Adapt 24-48 hours later | Trends often migrate TikTok → Instagram |
| Instagram aesthetic → TikTok | Less polished, more authentic | TikTok rewards rawness over polish |

### Combined Strategy for Developer Products

```
Week 1: Create 5 TikTok videos (educational + entertaining)
         → Adapt 3 best performers as Instagram Reels
         → Create 1 carousel from best educational video
         → Share behind-the-scenes in Stories

Week 2: Create 1 Instagram carousel (deep tutorial)
         → Adapt as TikTok video with narration
         → Create 3 more TikToks (tips, entertainment)
         → Stories: polls about next content

Week 3: Repeat Week 1 pattern
Week 4: Repeat Week 2 pattern + monthly analytics review
```
